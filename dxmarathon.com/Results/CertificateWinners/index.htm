CERTIFICATE WINNERS

Each year, the DX Marathon committee will award certificates to the top scorers for each country, CQ zone, mode and band.   Certificates will be mailed direct to the winners. 

Country winners had the highest score submitted in that country, regardless of Class.

Zone winners had the highest score submitted in that zone, regardless of Class.

Mode winners had the highest score submitted for a single mode (3 total) submission, regardless of Class.

Band winners had the highest score for a single band submission, regardless of Class.

U=Unlimited Class  F=Formula Class
