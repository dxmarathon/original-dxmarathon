CQ Magazine's Annual DX Marathon

Starting January 1 of each year, the DX Marathon is the perfect answer for the DX-er who needs that extra incentive to get on the air every day!  Simply work as many countries and CQ Zones as you can in each calendar year, regardless of the band or mode.  Each country and zone counts only once, so you can concentrate on working new ones rather than working the same ones on multiple bands and modes.   Awards are given for the top scores in 3 modes and 10 bands plus low power/small antennas.     Details on awards can be found here.   Need more information?  Contact us at: info@dxmarathon.com
