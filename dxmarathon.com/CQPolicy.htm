CQ Policy Statement for the 2022 DX Marathon

 

On March 17th 2022, CQ Communications, Inc., announced that it will not accept competitive entries in any of its sponsored contests by amateur radio stations in Russia, Belarus or the Donbas region of Ukraine.   Logs submitted by these stations will be accepted only as checklogs. In addition, contacts with these stations by other participants will have zero point value and will not count as multipliers.   This decision applies to all CQ sponsored contests, including the DX Marathon.  

As the DX Marathon is a year long contest activity, this note is to clarify how the CQ announcement affects the DX Marathon.    Any contacts made before March 26th, 2022 with stations from Russia or Belarus will count for the 2022 DX Marathon.  Contacts with Russia or Belarus made on or after March 26th, 2022 will not count for the 2022 DX Marathon.    If a station from Russia or Belarus submits a log for the 2022 DX Marathon, only contacts made before March 26th, 2022 will count for credit.
