Logs Received for 2014 Marathon

 

Below is a list of logs that have been received.  If you sent your log to us and it does not appear in the list below, please contact the DX Marathon administrator at john@dxmarathon.com. 

 

Please check your submission carefully and make sure log is compliant as follows:

 

1. Must use the official scoresheet version 2014.1 or later

2. Must include all required information at the top of the form

3. Must include antenna description for Formula and Limited classes

4. Must not contain any formatting errors

 

Only logs meeting the above requirements can be processed and considered for the 2014 Marathon.

 

If your submission is missing required information, contains formatting errors or has been submitted using the wrong form, you will be notified and given an opportunity to correct your submission.  Notifications will made as soon as possible after submission.
