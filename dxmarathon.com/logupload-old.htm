2020 DX Marathon Log Upload

 

Important notes for users of AD1C's ADIF to DX Marathon software: 

- Always use the latest version of the software - latest version is 20

- Use the latest DX Marathon submission form - latest is 2020.1

- If you upload your log and receive a message that says "You do not have a valid QSO for country credit.
You do not have a valid QSO for zone credit."  please open your DX Marathon log file and then "save as" the same file name.  Upload again and it should be OK.

 

Once your log has been accepted it will be shown here.   It may take up to 24 hours for your call to appear in the list.  If your call does not appear within 24 hours of submitting your log, please contact k9el at dxmarathon.com.

 

If you discover you made an error on your submission, simply upload the new file and it will replace your previous submission.

 

A very special thanks to Mike, ND9G, for creating and implementing our online log entry.
