CQ Magazine's Annual DX Marathon

   2014 High Claimed Scores

 

 

The following are the high claimed scores as submitted for the 2014 edition of CQ Magazine's annual DX Marathon.  Scores may contain pirates, incorrect callsigns, incorrect zones, non-valid operations, antennas not approved for Limited or Formula Class and entry errors that may reduce any of these scores.  Final results of the 2014 DX Marathon will be published in the June 2015 issue of CQ Magazine and on this web site after July 1st, 2015.

 

In 2014, all 40 zones were active and 292 CQ countries were active for a maximum possible score of 332. 

For Phone, 286 countries and all 40 zones were active for a maximum possible score of 326.

For CW, 275 countries and all 40 zones were active for a maximum possible score of 315.

For Digital, 261 countries and all 40 zones were active for a maximum possible score of 301.
