CQ Magazine's Annual DX Marathon

   2015 High Claimed Scores

(last update 18Jan)

 

The following are the high claimed scores as submitted for the 2015 edition of CQ Magazine's annual DX Marathon.  Scores may contain pirates, incorrect callsigns, incorrect zones, non-valid operations, antennas not approved for Limited or Formula Class and entry errors that may reduce any of these scores.  Final results of the 2015 DX Marathon will be published in the June 2016 issue of CQ Magazine and on this web site after July 1st, 2016.

 

The maximum possible score in 2015 was 340.
