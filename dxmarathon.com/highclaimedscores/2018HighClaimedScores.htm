CQ Magazine's Annual DX Marathon

   2018 High Claimed Scores

 

 

The following are the high claimed scores as submitted for the 2018 edition of CQ Magazine's annual DX Marathon.  Scores may contain pirates, incorrect callsigns, incorrect zones, non-valid operations, antennas not approved for Limited or Formula Class and entry errors that may reduce any of these scores.  Final results of the 2018 DX Marathon will be published in the June 2019 issue of CQ Magazine and on this web site after June 1st, 2019.

 

The maximum possible score in 2018 was 333 for mixed mode.
